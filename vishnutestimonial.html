Vishnu Menon As cliche as it sounds, Academic Decathlon has truly changed my life. As someone who can be rather lazy when it comes to school work, Decathlon has instilled in me the importance of developing a strong work ethic. Decathlon has taught me that the effort I personally put in not only affects me, but also my team, and as a result I have to work hard to not let them down.

The strong work ethic I developed carried over to my school life as well, as my grades have been significantly better upon joining Decathlon. However most importantly, Decathlon has created such a strong bond between my teammates and I, one that feels like a second family, and one that will last a lifetime.
