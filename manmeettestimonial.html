Manmeet Singh I joined AcaDeca because I wanted to improve my speech and interview skills, but also to improve my interpersonal skills. AcaDeca was a very valuable experience for me because it became a home away from home. As a team, we spent countless hours together. Even when we were studying, we made it bearable by making jokes out of the material.

The most enjoyable part of being on the AcaDeca team for me was when we initially started practicing our speeches and interviews. Especially for interviews and the impromptus, I remember not being able to talk for 30 seconds straight without stuttering or saying “umm” every 5 seconds. Even though it was a struggle in the beginning, after practicing and helping each other get better, impromptus and interviews became my favorite events.

Overall, I think Acadeca is an amazing experience as long as you’re willing to become a part of the team and improve, even slightly, not for yourself but for the team.
