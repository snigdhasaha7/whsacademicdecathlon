Irene Koo Academic Decathlon was an invaluable part in not only my time in high school, but also my overall learning experience. I loved the opportunity to learn deeply about a variety of subjects centered on a topic, especially as it allowed me to study beyond what normal school curricula covers. The subjective portions of decathlon were also immensely helpful - preparing for the interview, speech, and essay all proved to be foundational in school, interviews, and my career.

Last but not least, the most valuable and special aspect of being a part of the Woodbridge Decathlon team was working with the team. Spending many hours of training could be grueling, but the team culture was supportive and like a family - teaching each other, many shared inside jokes and snacks, and pushing one another to grow. I absolutely loved my time as a decathlete and really missed it after I graduated (so I went back and worked as an assistant coach :)).
