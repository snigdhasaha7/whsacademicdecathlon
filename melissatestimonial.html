Melissa Tsaowimonsiri It’s hard to pare down all that I have to say about Academic Decathlon. While I’ve always loved seeking out random bits of knowledge, I cannot say the same about coming up with speeches almost instantaneously. Decathlon has armed me with knowledge and new skills, like speech improvisation skills or interview techniques.

But more, much more than this, it has strengthened my friendships. I have gotten closer to my entire team and experienced innumerable moments that I will treasure for eternity. From studying with my teammates, to texting them until midnight, to eating dinner with them, it has always been an amazing and unforgettable ride. For a person who is shy or who doesn’t have many friends, Decathlon is such a valuable opportunity to create connections. Decathlon is one of the best activities I have ever participated in.
